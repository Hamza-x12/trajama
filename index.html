About Moroccan Darija

Darija (الدارجة) is the colloquial Arabic dialect spoken in Morocco by over 30 million people. It differs significantly from Modern Standard Arabic and incorporates influences from Berber, French, and Spanish. Tarjama specializes in understanding these unique linguistic nuances to provide accurate translations that preserve the original meaning and cultural context.
